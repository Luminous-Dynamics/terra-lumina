## Stage 4: Verification (KYC/AML)

### When Verification is Required

**Trigger**: User clicks "Invest" on any project

**Process:**
1. User redirected to verification flow
2. Identity verification (KYC)
3. Accreditation verification (if needed for Reg D)
4. AML screening (automated)
5. Manual review (if flagged)
6. Approval notification

### Identity Verification (KYC)

**Provider**: Plaid, Stripe Identity, or Jumio

**Information Required:**
- Full legal name
- Date of birth
- Address (residential)
- Social Security Number or Tax ID
- Government-issued ID (photo upload)
  - Driver's license, Passport, or State ID

**Verification Process:**
1. Enter information in secure form
2. Upload ID photo (front and back)
3. Selfie for biometric matching
4. Automated verification (90% instant approval)
5. Manual review if flagged (24-48 hours)

**Pass Rate Target**: 95%

### Accreditation Verification (Reg D Only)

**Required For:**
- Reg D 506(c) offerings (accredited investors only)
- Investments >$100K (even in Reg CF)

**Accreditation Criteria:**
- Income >$200K/year (individual) or $300K (joint) for 2+ years
- Net worth >$1M (excluding primary residence)
- Professional certifications (Series 7, 65, 82)

**Verification Methods:**

**Option 1: Self-Certification** (Reg CF)
- Investor attests to accreditation status
- Platform relies on attestation
- Risk: Investor misrepresentation

**Option 2: Third-Party Verification** (Reg D)
- Upload tax returns (last 2 years)
- Upload W-2s or bank statements
- CPA letter confirming accreditation
- Third-party service (e.g., VerifyInvestor)

**Timeline**: 1-3 business days

### AML Screening

**Automated Checks:**
- OFAC sanctions list
- PEP (Politically Exposed Persons) database
- Adverse media screening
- Fraud databases

**Red Flags:**
- Sanctioned individual or entity
- High-risk country of residence
- PEP without sufficient due diligence
- Suspicious transaction patterns

**Manual Review Triggers:**
- Any AML flag
- Large investment amount (>$100K)
- Unusual funding source
- Incomplete information

**Review Timeline**: 1-2 business days

### Verification Status

**Statuses:**
- **Pending**: Verification in progress
- **Approved**: Clear to invest
- **Needs Info**: Missing documents or information
- **Under Review**: Manual review in progress
- **Denied**: Failed verification (rare, <1%)

**Communication:**
- Email notification for each status change
- In-app notification
- Clear next steps if action required

## Stage 5: First Investment

### Investment Process (Step-by-Step)

**Step 1: Select Project**
- User browses projects
- Clicks "Invest" on chosen project
- Redirected to investment flow

**Step 2: Choose Amount**
- Minimum investment displayed (typically $25-$1,000)
- Input custom amount or select preset ($1K, $5K, $10K)
- Real-time calculation of:
  - Ownership percentage
  - Projected annual return
  - Total projected return over life
- Reg CF limit check (if applicable)

**Step 3: Review Investment**
- Project summary
- Investment amount and terms
- Fees (2.5% platform fee, 1% annual success fee)
- Risks and disclosures
- Checkbox: "I have read and understand the risks"

**Step 4: Review Legal Documents**
- Investment agreement (SAFE, equity, or note)
- Subscription agreement
- Risk disclosures
- Option to download PDF
- Checkbox: "I agree to the investment terms"

**Step 5: E-Signature**
- DocuSign integration
- Click to sign electronically
- Date and timestamp recorded

**Step 6: Payment**
- Payment methods:
  - Bank transfer (ACH) - preferred, no fees
  - Wire transfer - for large amounts
  - Credit/debit card - 3% fee (discouraged)
- Payment instructions displayed
- Payment submitted to Stripe

**Step 7: Confirmation**
- Investment confirmation page
- Confirmation email sent immediately
- Receipt emailed (for tax purposes)
- Investment shows in dashboard as "Pending"

**Timeline**: 10-15 minutes for entire process

### Payment Processing

**ACH (Automated Clearing House):**
- 3-5 business days to clear
- No fees to investor
- Preferred method for investments <$50K

**Wire Transfer:**
- Same-day or next-day
- $15-$30 fee (investor pays)
- Preferred for investments >$50K

**Credit/Debit Card:**
- Instant
- 3% processing fee (investor pays)
- Limited to $10K per transaction
- Discouraged due to fees

**Payment Confirmation:**
- Email when payment received
- Investment status updates to "Funded"
- Investor dashboard updated

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Issue: Low Signup Conversion

**Symptoms**: <5% of visitors signup
**Diagnosis**: Landing page not compelling or trustworthy
**Solutions:**
- Add social proof (investor count, $ deployed)
- Simplify signup form (fewer fields)
- Add trust badges (security, compliance)
- Improve value proposition clarity
- A/B test headlines and CTAs

---

### Issue: High Drop-Off at Verification

**Symptoms**: <70% complete KYC
**Diagnosis**: Verification too complex or invasive
**Solutions:**
- Simplify KYC flow (fewer steps)
- Explain why verification is required
- Offer manual verification alternative
- Provide support during verification
- Test different KYC providers

---

### Issue: Long Time to First Investment

**Symptoms**: >30 days median
**Diagnosis**: Investors not educated or confident
**Solutions:**
- Accelerate education (shorter videos, better FAQ)
- Add live chat for questions
- Offer 1-on-1 onboarding calls
- Create "Quick Start" guide
- Send targeted nudge emails

---

### Issue: Low Repeat Investment Rate

**Symptoms**: <40% make 2nd investment
**Diagnosis**: Poor first experience or lack of engagement
**Solutions:**
- Improve post-investment communication
- Highlight new projects to existing investors
- Offer early access or discounts for repeat investors
- Better project recommendations
- Address satisfaction issues (NPS detractors)
