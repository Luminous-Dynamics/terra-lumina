## Overview

This data room contains comprehensive due diligence materials for potential investors in Terra Lumina's Pre-Seed funding round. All materials are confidential and should not be distributed without explicit permission.

### Access Protocol

**Who Gets Access:**
- Investors who have expressed serious interest (soft commitment or meeting scheduled)
- Accredited investors only (until Reg CF offering live)
- Strategic partners evaluating investment

**Access Request Process:**
1. Email invest@luminousdynamics.org with:
   - Your name and organization
   - Investment interest level
   - Accredited investor status
   - Any specific questions or focus areas
2. NDA signature (for detailed financials and sensitive materials)
3. Receive secure link via email within 24 hours
4. Access granted for 30 days (renewable)

**Tracking:**
- All document views tracked via analytics
- Follow up within 48 hours of access
- Monthly access audit

## FAQ for Data Room Users

**Q: Can I share these materials with my partners/co-investors?**
A: Basic materials (pitch deck, one-pager) - yes, with discretion. Detailed materials (financial model, contracts) - only with signed NDA and our approval.

**Q: How current are these documents?**
A: All documents show "Last Updated" date. Financials and traction updated monthly minimum. Ping us if you need the latest version.

**Q: Can I download the financial model to run my own scenarios?**
A: Yes, email us and we'll send editable version. We're happy to walk through assumptions.

**Q: What if I have questions not answered in these docs?**
A: Email invest@luminousdynamics.org or schedule a call. We love questions - shows you're doing deep diligence.

**Q: How long do I have access?**
A: 30 days default. Just ask if you need more time. We want you to have everything you need.

**Q: Are there any documents you're NOT sharing?**
A: We're radically transparent. Only exclusions: personally identifiable info (employee SSNs, etc.) and anything that violates third-party NDAs. If you need something specific, ask.

**Q: Can I visit the team or see a demo?**
A: Absolutely! We're remote-first, but happy to do video calls or meet in person if you're local. Platform demo available upon request (currently wireframes, MVP live Month 3).
