Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common investor questions

Why Iceland specifically? +
Iceland offers the world's most affordable renewable energy at $0.02-0.03/kWh from geothermal sources, proven technology with 90+ years of operation, stable democracy with strong property rights, and strategic location between North America and Europe. The country already powers 100% of its grid with renewables and has excess capacity ready for development.
What makes the 18-22% IRR achievable? +
Our returns are driven by: energy arbitrage ($0.02 production vs $0.15 market rate), industrial anchor tenants (data centers paying premium rates), real estate appreciation in energy-abundant zones, carbon credit revenue streams, and government incentives for sustainable development. These are conservative projections based on existing Iceland energy projects.
Why $750K-1.5M for Pre-Seed? +
The pre-seed funding will enable us to: build and launch the Terra Atlas investment platform, develop initial project pipeline and partnerships, conduct feasibility studies for pilot projects, establish legal structures for investment vehicles, and build the core team. This positions us for a $7-15M Series A in 18-24 months with proven traction.
What's the exit strategy? +
Multiple exit paths: IPO of the energy infrastructure (comparable to Orsted's $30B valuation), strategic acquisition by energy majors or sovereign wealth funds, REIT conversion for the real estate portfolio, or long-term dividend yields of 8-12% annually. The infrastructure nature provides stable, predictable returns.
How do you handle construction in Iceland's climate? +
Iceland has 90+ years of experience building in Arctic conditions. We use proven techniques: reinforced foundations for volcanic soil, geothermal heating for year-round construction, prefabricated components manufactured off-site, and summer intensification for major works. Our construction partners have delivered 500+ projects in Iceland.
