## Email Best Practices

### Timing
- Respond to inquiries within 24 hours
- Send updates on same day/time monthly
- Follow up after calls within 24 hours

### Tone
- Professional but personal
- Transparent about challenges
- Grateful for their time
- Confident but not arrogant

### Structure
- Clear subject lines
- Short paragraphs (2-3 lines max)
- Bullet points for scanability
- Bold for emphasis
- Clear calls-to-action

### Legal
- Don't make projections sound guaranteed
- Include forward-looking statement disclaimer if needed
- Don't share material non-public information inappropriately
- Keep all investor communications documented
