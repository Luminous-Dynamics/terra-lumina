## Revenue Model

### Primary Revenue Streams

**1. Platform Fees (60% of revenue)**
- Transaction fee: 2-3% on all investments
- Average blended rate: 2.5%
- Paid by investors at time of investment

**2. Success Fees (25% of revenue)**
- Performance fee: 1% annually on funded projects
- Paid by project developers from returns
- Aligns incentives with project success

**3. Premium Services (10% of revenue)**
- Data room access: $500-2,000/month for developers
- Advanced analytics: $200-500/month for investors
- White-label solutions: Custom pricing

**4. Partnerships & Affiliates (5% of revenue)**
- Financial institution referrals
- Insurance partnerships
- Legal service referrals

## Risk Mitigation Strategies

### Financial Risks

**Slower Growth Than Projected**
- Mitigation: Conservative assumptions, bridge funding options identified
- Trigger: 3 consecutive months <80% of projections
- Action: Reduce burn by 30%, extend runway

**Fee Compression (Competition)**
- Mitigation: Premium services, superior UX, network effects
- Trigger: Competitor launches at <2% fees
- Action: Enhance value-add services, tiered pricing

**Regulatory Costs Higher Than Expected**
- Mitigation: $175K Year 1 legal budget, expert advisors
- Trigger: New securities regulations
- Action: Pivot to advisory model, partnership structures

**Extended Time to Profitability**
- Mitigation: Strong runway management, multiple funding rounds
- Trigger: Break-even delayed >2 quarters
- Action: Series B earlier, cost optimization

## Appendix: Financial Model Methodology

### Revenue Modeling
- Bottom-up: Project pipeline × conversion rate × average size × fee
- Top-down: Market size × penetration rate
- Validation: Comparable platforms (crowdfunding, real estate)

### Expense Modeling
- Team: Market rate salaries + 30% benefits/taxes
- Development: Contractor rates + infrastructure costs
- Marketing: Industry benchmarks (20-25% of revenue in growth phase)

### Assumptions Validation
- Investor surveys: 200+ respondents on pricing sensitivity
- Developer interviews: 40+ project developers on fee tolerance
- Market research: 3rd party reports on renewable investment trends
- Comparable analysis: 12 similar platforms analyzed
