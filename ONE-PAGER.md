## The Opportunity in 60 Seconds

Terra Lumina is building the **platform that democratizes energy infrastructure investment**. We connect investors of all sizes ($100 to $10M) with vetted renewable energy projects worldwide.

**The Problem**: $4 trillion needed for energy transition, but individual investors are locked out. Institutional minimums ($10M+) exclude 99% of potential investors.

**Our Solution**: Investment platform enabling anyone to invest in renewable energy projects with transparent returns (11-14% target IRR) and positive impact.
