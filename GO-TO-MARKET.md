## Customer Acquisition Strategy

### Acquisition Funnel

```
Awareness (Top of Funnel)
   ↓ 10,000 website visitors/month
Consideration (Middle of Funnel)
   ↓ 1,000 email signups (10% conversion)
Intent (Bottom of Funnel)
   ↓ 250 account creations (25% conversion)
Investment (Conversion)
   ↓ 50 first-time investors (20% conversion)
   ↓ 0.5% overall conversion rate
```

### Year 1 Targets

**Website Traffic**: 60,000 unique visitors
**Email List**: 6,000 subscribers
**Registered Users**: 1,500 accounts
**Active Investors**: 100-150

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

**Target CAC**
- Year 1: $2,000-$2,500 per investor
- Year 2: $1,000-$1,500 per investor
- Year 3: $500-$800 per investor

**CAC by Channel** (estimates)
- Content marketing: $800-$1,200
- Paid advertising: $2,500-$3,500
- Partnerships: $500-$1,000
- Referrals: $200-$400
- PR/earned media: $300-$800

### Lifetime Value (LTV)

**Average Investor**
- Investments: 3.2 over 7-year period
- Average investment: $7,500
- Platform fee (2.5%): $187.50 per investment
- Total LTV: $600 (platform fees only)

**Including Success Fees**
- Success fees (1% annually × 5 years): $375 per investment
- Total LTV with success fees: $1,800

**LTV:CAC Target**: 3:1 minimum (achieved by Year 2)

## Pricing & Economics

### Investor Fees

**Platform Fee**: 2.5% of investment amount
- Industry benchmark: 1-5%
- Justification: Lower than alternatives (Fundrise 3-5%), covers compliance costs
- Waived for investments >$100K (negotiate custom)

**Success Fee**: 1% annually on project returns
- Aligns incentives (only earn when investors earn)
- Performance-based fee structure
- Covers ongoing platform support and reporting

**No Management Fees**: Unlike ETFs or mutual funds
**No Exit Fees**: No fees to exit via community ownership transition

### Developer Fees

**Listing Fee**: $0 (free to list)
- Lower barrier to entry
- Attract more projects
- Revenue from funded projects only

**Platform Fee**: 5% of funds raised (one-time)
- Industry benchmark: 3-7%
- Includes: Marketing, legal support, investor relations
- Paid upon successful funding

**Optional Services** (Premium)
- Due diligence support: $5,000-$10,000
- Enhanced marketing: $2,000-$5,000
- Legal document preparation: $3,000-$8,000

## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Low Conversion Rates

**Mitigation**
- A/B test all marketing materials
- Extensive user testing and feedback
- Simplify onboarding process
- Reduce minimum investment to lower barrier

---

### Risk 2: High CAC

**Mitigation**
- Focus on organic channels (content, referrals)
- Strict paid ad ROI requirements (3:1 minimum)
- Partnerships for customer acquisition

---

### Risk 3: Investor Education Barrier

**Mitigation**
- Comprehensive educational content
- Live webinars and Q&A sessions
- Simple, transparent platform design
- 1-on-1 support for new investors

---

### Risk 4: Trust & Credibility

**Mitigation**
- Radical transparency (monthly reports)
- Third-party security audits
- Strong regulatory compliance
- Investor testimonials and case studies
