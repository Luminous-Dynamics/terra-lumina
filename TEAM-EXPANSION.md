## Compensation Philosophy

### Principles

**1. Competitive but Not Top-of-Market**
- Target: 50th-75th percentile of market rates
- Equity makes up the gap to top-of-market total comp
- Location-adjusted (SF/NY higher than other cities)

**2. Equity for All**
- Every employee receives equity (options)
- More equity for earlier hires (higher risk)
- 4-year vesting, 1-year cliff standard

**3. Transparent & Fair**
- Salary bands published internally
- Consistent leveling framework
- No negotiation games (make best offer first)

**4. Performance-Based**
- Annual raises based on performance and market
- Bonuses for roles with clear revenue impact
- Equity refreshes for top performers

---

### Recruiting Process

**Stage 1: Application (Day 0)**
- Online application with resume + cover letter
- Optional: Portfolio, GitHub, writing samples
- Response within 3 business days

**Stage 2: Phone Screen (Day 3-5)**
- 30-minute call with hiring manager or recruiter
- Culture fit, motivation, basic qualifications
- Decide: Advance, decline, or hold for future

**Stage 3: Technical/Skills Assessment (Day 7-10)**
- Engineers: Take-home coding challenge (4-6 hours max)
- Business roles: Case study or presentation
- All roles: Paid work sample if >4 hours

**Stage 4: Team Interviews (Day 14-17)**
- 3-4 interviews (45-60 min each)
- Hiring manager, team members, cross-functional
- Focus: Skills, culture fit, mission alignment

**Stage 5: Final Interview (Day 20-22)**
- CEO or C-level interview
- Vision alignment, leadership potential
- Two-way conversation (candidate asks questions too)

**Stage 6: Offer (Day 24-25)**
- Verbal offer (phone)
- Written offer within 24 hours
- 1 week to decide (negotiable)

**Stage 7: Onboarding (Week 1)**
- Equipment shipped before start date
- Day 1 onboarding schedule
- 30-60-90 day plan

**Total Timeline**: ~4 weeks from application to start (expedited for critical roles)
