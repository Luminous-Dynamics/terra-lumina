## Executive Summary

### Risk Management Framework

Terra Lumina employs a **proactive, transparent** approach to risk management:

1. **Identify**: Comprehensive risk mapping across all business areas
2. **Assess**: Probability and impact scoring for each risk
3. **Mitigate**: Specific action plans to reduce likelihood and impact
4. **Monitor**: Continuous tracking with quarterly formal reviews
5. **Communicate**: Transparent disclosure to investors and stakeholders

### Risk Appetite

As a Pre-Seed startup in a regulated industry, we maintain:
- **Low tolerance**: Regulatory and legal risks
- **Medium tolerance**: Market and competitive risks
- **Higher tolerance**: Product and technical risks (calculated experimentation)

### Top 5 Risks

1. **Regulatory Changes** (High Impact, Low-Medium Probability)
2. **Project Failures** (High Impact, Medium Probability)
3. **Slow Adoption** (High Impact, Medium Probability)
4. **Security Breach** (High Impact, Low Probability)

## Risk Scoring Matrix

**Probability Scale**
- **Low**: <20% chance in next 12 months
- **Medium**: 20-50% chance in next 12 months
- **High**: >50% chance in next 12 months

**Impact Scale**
- **Low**: <$50K impact or <10% operational disruption
- **Medium**: $50K-$500K impact or 10-30% operational disruption
- **High**: >$500K impact or >30% operational disruption
- **Critical**: Existential threat to company

**Risk Level** = Probability × Impact
- **Critical**: Immediate action required
- **High**: Active mitigation in progress
- **Medium**: Monitored with contingency plans
- **Low**: Monitored only

## Regulatory & Legal Risks

### Risk 1.1: Securities Regulation Changes

**Risk**: SEC changes rules for Reg CF, Reg D, or Reg A+, increasing compliance costs or restricting offerings

**Probability**: Medium (30%)
**Impact**: High
**Risk Level**: HIGH

**Current Mitigations**
- Built platform with flexibility to adapt to multiple regulatory frameworks
- Engaged securities lawyer with deep SEC expertise
- Monitoring regulatory changes via legal counsel and industry associations
- Over-comply with current regulations to buffer against changes

**Additional Mitigations**
- Maintain 6-month legal reserve fund ($100K+)
- Diversify offering types (Reg CF, Reg D, Reg A+)
- Build relationships with SEC staff (request no-action letters when appropriate)
- Join FINRA as broker-dealer alternative if needed

**Contingency Plan**
- If Reg CF eliminated: Pivot to Reg D 506(c) for accredited-only
- If major cost increase: Raise prices, cut features, or seek Series A earlier

**Monitoring**: Monthly review of SEC proposed rules, quarterly legal review

---

### Risk 1.2: State Securities (Blue Sky) Compliance

**Risk**: State-by-state securities registration requirements overwhelm resources

**Probability**: Medium (40%)
**Impact**: Medium
**Risk Level**: MEDIUM-HIGH

**Current Mitigations**
- Reg CF federal preemption (no state review for Reg CF offerings)
- Limiting to Reg D 506(c) initially (federal preemption)
- Legal budget includes state filing fees

**Additional Mitigations**
- Use filing services (e.g., CrowdCheck) to manage state filings
- Prioritize states with largest investor bases (CA, NY, TX, FL)
- Consider Reg A+ (Tier 2) for larger offerings (federal preemption)

**Contingency Plan**
- If costs too high: Limit investor access by state
- Partner with broker-dealer for filing support

**Monitoring**: Track filing costs and time by state quarterly

---

### Risk 1.3: Platform Liability for Project Performance

**Risk**: Investors sue Terra Lumina for project failures or misrepresentations

**Probability**: Medium (35%)
**Impact**: High
**Risk Level**: HIGH

**Current Mitigations**
- Strong disclaimer language in all materials
- Thorough project due diligence process
- Investor accreditation and suitability checks
- Errors & Omissions insurance ($1M-$2M coverage)
- Legal review of all project materials

**Additional Mitigations**
- Implement investor education requirements (quiz before first investment)
- Invest-only-what-you-can-afford-to-lose prompts
- Third-party project ratings (independent due diligence)
- Arbitration clauses in terms of service

**Contingency Plan**
- Legal defense fund ($250K reserved)
- Insurance claims process
- Settlement negotiations
- Platform improvements based on learnings

**Monitoring**: Legal consult on all new projects, quarterly insurance review

### Risk 1.4: Anti-Money Laundering (AML) Violations

**Risk**: Platform used for money laundering, leading to fines or shutdown

**Probability**: Low (15%)
**Impact**: Critical
**Risk Level**: MEDIUM

**Current Mitigations**
- KYC verification for all investors (ID verification)
- Transaction monitoring (flag large/unusual transactions)
- AML compliance officer (outsourced initially)
- Suspicious Activity Report (SAR) procedures
- Ongoing training for team

**Additional Mitigations**
- Enhanced due diligence for large investors (>$25K)
- Source of funds documentation for large investments
- Third-party AML screening (e.g., ComplyAdvantage)
- Regular AML audits

**Contingency Plan**
- File SARs immediately if suspicious activity detected
- Cooperate fully with FinCEN investigation
- Enhanced controls implementation

**Monitoring**: Monthly transaction review, quarterly AML policy review

**Risk**: Platform hacked, investor data stolen, or ransomware attack

**Probability**: Low (15%)
**Impact**: Critical
**Risk Level**: HIGH

**Current Mitigations**
- Encryption at rest and in transit (AES-256, TLS 1.3)
- Regular security audits (quarterly)
- Penetration testing (annually)
- SOC 2 compliance roadmap
- Cyber insurance ($1M-$2M coverage)

**Additional Mitigations**
- Multi-factor authentication required
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Regular security training for team
- Incident response plan
- Bug bounty program (Year 2)

**Contingency Plan**
- Immediate incident response (isolate, assess, notify)
- Engage cybersecurity firm for forensics
- Notify affected users within 72 hours (GDPR requirement)
- Law enforcement notification if criminal activity
- Remediation and enhanced security measures

**Monitoring**: Real-time security monitoring, quarterly audit reports
