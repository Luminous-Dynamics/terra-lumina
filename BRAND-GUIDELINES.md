1. **Transparency** - Radical honesty about progress, challenges, and risks
2. **Accessibility** - Low barriers, clear language, inclusive design
3. **Impact** - Measurable environmental and social benefit
4. **Community** - Investor-first, mission-aligned decision making

- **Professional** yet approachable

- **Optimistic** without being unrealistic
- **Technical** without being jargon-heavy
- **Mission-driven** without being preachy

**We are**:

- Professionally optimistic
- Transparently honest

- Genuinely helpful

- Patient and clear

- Professional and respectful

- "Here's what we accomplished..." "Here's where we need to improve..."

- Benefit-focused

## Writing Guidelines

### General Rules

**Do**:
- Write in active voice ("We're building..." not "The platform is being built...")
- Use specific numbers ("87,000 dams" not "many dams")
- Break up long paragraphs (2-3 sentences max online)
- Use bullet points for lists
- Bold key points
- Link to additional resources

**Don't**:
- Use jargon without explanation
- Make guarantees about returns
- Overuse exclamation points!!!
- Write walls of text
- Assume reader knowledge

### Word Choice

**Use** ‚Üí **Instead of**:
- Investor ‚Üí Customer, user
- Platform ‚Üí App, website
- Renewable energy ‚Üí Green energy, clean tech
- Infrastructure ‚Üí Facilities, equipment
- Democratize ‚Üí Make accessible
- Community-owned ‚Üí Shared, collective
- Transparent ‚Üí Open, honest
- Impact ‚Üí Change, difference

**Preferred Terms**:
- "We're" vs "we are" (conversational)
- "Can't" vs "cannot" (natural)
- "$100" vs "one hundred dollars" (clear)
- "11-14%" vs "eleven to fourteen percent" (scannable)

### Investment-Specific Language

**Always Include Disclaimers**:
- "Target returns" not "guaranteed returns"
- "Historical performance" not "expected performance"
- "Projected" not "will be"
- Always add: "Past performance doesn't guarantee future results"
- Always add: "All investments carry risk"

**Compliant Language**:
‚úÖ "We target 11-14% IRR based on comparable projects"
‚ùå "You'll earn 11-14% returns"

‚úÖ "Investment opportunities for accredited investors"
‚ùå "Anyone can invest" (not true for Pre-Seed)

‚úÖ "This is not investment advice. Consult your advisor."

## Channel-Specific Guidelines

### Website

**Homepage**:
- Clear value proposition above fold
- Primary CTA visible immediately
- Secondary CTAs for different audiences
- Social proof (when available)
- Clean, modern design

**Content Pages**:
- Scannable (headers, bullets, short paragraphs)
- Clear hierarchy (H1 ‚Üí H2 ‚Üí H3)
- Visuals every 200-300 words
- CTAs throughout (not just at end)

### Email

**Subject Lines**:
- 40-50 characters max
- Front-load important words
- Avoid spam triggers
- A/B test when possible

Examples:
‚úÖ "Terra Lumina Update - January 2025"
‚úÖ "Quick question about your investment"
‚ùå "RE: RE: RE: Following up"
‚ùå "FREE MONEY!!! INVEST NOW!!!"

**Body**:
- Personalize when possible
- One clear CTA per email
- Mobile-friendly (short paragraphs)
- Professional signature

### Social Media

**Twitter/X**:
- Concise, impactful
- Use threads for longer content
- Hashtags: #CleanEnergy #ImpactInvesting #RenewableEnergy
- Link to blog/resources

**LinkedIn**:
- Professional, detailed
- Industry insights
- Company updates
- Thought leadership

**Tone**: Professional optimism, data-driven, solution-focused

### Investor Updates

**Monthly Email Format**:
1. Top-line summary (3 bullets)
2. Key metrics (numbers table)
3. Wins this month (3-5 items)
4. Challenges (1-2 items with how addressing)
5. Next month goals
6. How investors can help

**Tone**: Transparent, specific, balanced (wins + challenges)
